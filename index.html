What’s Shastra Want
to Give You?

Shastra Technical Institute aims to empower aspiring engineers and Kerala PSC candidates with the knowledge, skills, and confidence to excel in competitive technical exams and academic pursuits. Specifically, Shastra offers:

Tailored coaching for Kerala PSC Technical Exams (e.g., Polytechnic Lecturer, Assistant Professor), UPSC, SSC JE, ISRO, DRDO, and other central government exams.

Expertly crafted MCQ books, theory books, mock tests, and video lectures designed to simplify complex concepts.

Access to a YouTube channel with 45,000+ subscribers, offering free video lectures, topic-wise discussions, and doubt-clearing sessions for KTU B.Tech and M.Tech students.

A track record of over 400 Kerala PSC appointments, ensuring you’re equipped to achieve your career goals.

Structured online and offline batches, expert mentoring, and a student-focused approach to ensure conceptual clarity and exam readiness.
